Elon Musk | Jeff Bezos

Elon Musk

Elon Musk: “When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor.”

  • 1995: Dropped out of Standford after 2 days and started Zip2
  • 1999: Co-founded X.com more known as Paypal
  • 2001: Started project to make reusable rockets called SpaceX
  • 2003: Joined Tesla board as chairman

  • To get more information on Elon Musk check out the wikipedia page

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos: “One of the only ways to get out of a tight box is to invent your way out”

  • 1986: Graduated from Princeton University
  • 1993: Established an online bookstore and founded Amazon originally named Cadabra
  • 1996: Took Amazon public with an initial public offering
  • 2000: Founded Blue Origin, a human spaceflight startup
  • 2013: Purchased Washington Post for $250 million in cash

  • To get more information on Jeff Bezos check out the wikipedia page
